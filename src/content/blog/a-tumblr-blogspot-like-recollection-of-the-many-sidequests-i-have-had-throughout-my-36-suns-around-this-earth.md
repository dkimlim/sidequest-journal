I've been playing around with the idea of starting an online journal of my thoughts and projects, but I couldn't commit to starting a substack where I need to update "subscribers" weekly or monthly (consistency has been a lifelong kryptonite of mine iykyk). I'm more of a free-form sporadic thinker, and I really wanted to recreate this old blogspot I had called [Decipher Diane](https://decipherdiane.blogspot.com/) where I just dropped random musings of my daily life.

The time was 2010 and I was just starting fashion school and documenting outfit posts, and then later in 2013 started my new internship at an e-commerce start-up Frank & Oak, marrying my love for fashion and tech fo the first time. The last post in 2016 was right after I left Frank & Oak and was feeling all moody and hurt about it haha.

Fast-forward to 2018 (I'm filling in the blanks now), I completed a web development bootcamp and learnt about making apps and websites from back-end to front-end, then actually worked in tech until the pandemmy. Started a freelance career and burnt out with ridiculously terrible mental health and bad lifestyle habits. Then, slowly recovered and healed physically by taking on part-time work and contracts to not overburden myself, fell in love and started a new full-time job in tech & client management at a saas company. 

And here we are in 2026 with a refreshed new outlook on life and career, and a new online journal I vibe coded with the help of Claude Code (sorry for all the AI haters out there).

Anyway, join along on my learnings, random thoughts and possible outfit posts (probably not haha, just find me on instagram for that duh). 

x diane x
